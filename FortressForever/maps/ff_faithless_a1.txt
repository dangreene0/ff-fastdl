Fortress Forever remake of UT99 CTFFace

made by [Gumbuk 9 / Soap Breaker] over the course of 2 months
You've Got Mail!

custom UT99 style CTF style gamemode
take the enemy Plaque, bring it to your own when it's home

5 team points per capture - 400 player points per capture
