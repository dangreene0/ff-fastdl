Objectives:
- Pick up the flag at the middle of the map and hold it in the marked Hold Area.

Tips:
- You gain Quad Damage and your health and armor will regenerate while holding the flag.
- The health regeneration is percentual and varies between 5% and 10% depending on the number of players on the server.
- You can take the flag outside the Hold Area for 10 seconds. After that the flag returns to the middle.
